FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a typical IoT deployment take?

Small deployments (under 50 devices) typically go live in 4–6 weeks. Large enterprise projects run 3–6 months including integration and staff training.

Do you provide hardware, or just software?

We're a full-stack IoT provider — we source, configure, and install hardware, build the data platform, and provide ongoing managed services.

Can you integrate with our existing systems?

Yes. We integrate with most major ERP, CMMS, BMS, and cloud platforms via REST APIs, MQTT, OPC-UA, or custom connectors.

What happens if sensors go offline or fail?

Our platform detects device failures instantly and triggers alerts. We offer 4-hour on-site response SLAs for critical systems and remote diagnostics 24/7.

We're always happy to talk IoT. Reach out anytime.
